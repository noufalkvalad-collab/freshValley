Born in the deep forests of Wayanad

Generations of tribal gatherers have walked the dense, mist-covered canopies of Kerala's Western Ghats. Their knowledge of the forest is ancient, their respect for the bees, absolute.

Bee Kiss is not farmed. It is wild-harvested from naturally occurring hives high in the canopy, ensuring the bees' natural diet of rare medicinal flora remains undisturbed. This is honey as nature intended—thick, dark, complex, and profoundly nourishing.

The Truth About Crystallization

If your Bee Kiss honey crystallizes, celebrate! It is the ultimate proof of purity.

Real, raw honey naturally solidifies over time due to its high natural glucose content and presence of beneficial pollen. Only highly processed, heated, and ultra-filtered commercial honey stays perfectly liquid forever.

To return crystallized honey to liquid state, simply place the jar in warm water. Never microwave.
